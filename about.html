ABOUT US

At FurrY Pet Shop, we believe that pets are not just animals, but members of the family!

WHO WE ARE?

Who We Are: FurrY Petshop is a family-owned business dedicated to providing high-quality pet products and services. Our mission is to help pet owners take the best possible care of their furry family members. We believe that pets bring joy and love into our lives, and we want to make sure they receive the same in return. We take pride in offering a wide selection of products, from food and treats to toys and accessories, all carefully chosen to meet the needs of pets of all shapes and sizes. Our team of knowledgeable and friendly staff are passionate about animals and are always on hand to offer expert advice and recommendations. At FurrY Petshop, we understand that pets are unique and have individual needs, which is why we offer personalized services, such as grooming and training, to ensure that each pet receives the care and attention they require.

We are committed to being a responsible business, and we strive to minimize our environmental impact by using eco-friendly products and packaging materials. Thank you for choosing FurrY Petshop. We look forward to helping you provide the best possible care for your furry friends.
